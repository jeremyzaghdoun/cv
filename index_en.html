Roni Conseil

At the crossroads of IT and financial expertise

Roni Conseil was born from a clear vision: to combine technological expertise with deep knowledge of financial products in order to build digital solutions that truly reflect the realities of the financial sector.


After more than 20 years working in banking and insurance, I observed a persistent gap between technical teams and the specifics of financial products. Web and mobile projects often lacked a deep understanding of financial instruments, regulation, and business constraints.


Roni Conseil brings this unique dual perspective: strong command of digital technologies combined with in-depth knowledge of wealth management products, investment funds, retirement savings, and complex financial mechanisms.

The Roni Conseil promise

Support financial institutions in their digital transformation projects by providing a rare dual expertise: web and mobile technologies centered on user experience, and a deep understanding of financial products and the industry.


This combination makes it possible to design solutions that are not only technically solid, but also fully aligned with regulatory requirements, business constraints, and end-user expectations in the financial sector.
