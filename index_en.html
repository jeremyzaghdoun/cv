At the Intersection of IT and Financial Expertise

Roni Conseil was born from a clear vision : combine technological expertise and deep knowledge of financial products to create digital solutions that truly meet the sector's needs.


After over 20 years working in the banking and insurance sectors, I observed a persistent gap between technical teams and the realities of financial products. Web and mobile projects often lacked this deep understanding of financial instruments, regulation, and specific business issues.


Roni Conseil brings this unique expertise : mastery of digital technologies combined with in-depth knowledge of wealth management products, investment funds, retirement savings, and complex financial mechanisms.
